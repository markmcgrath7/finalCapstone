Why this split?

Push-pull-legs (PPL) is a popular workout split that involves dividing the body into three training days: push muscles (chest, shoulders, and triceps), pull muscles (back and biceps), and legs. This allows for more focused training on specific muscle groups and better recovery, as well as avoiding overtraining certain muscle groups. It is also a good way to target all muscle groups evenly over the course of a week's training.
